Discover the entrance fees for the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham Alabama

The entrance fees for the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham Alabama vary depending on the visitor's age and residency. For residents of Birmingham, the admission cost is $8 for adults, $5 for seniors (age 65 and above), and $5 for children (ages 3-12). Non-residents of Birmingham can expect to pay $12 for adults, $7 for seniors, and $7 for children. However, it's important to note that children under the age of 3 can enter the garden for free.

In addition to these general admission fees, there are also discounts available for groups of 10 or more people. For these group visits, the cost per person is reduced to $5 for both residents and non-residents. Furthermore, there are special rates for school groups, with prices starting at $2 per student. These fees include access to all the main attractions and exhibitions within the Botanical Gardens, providing visitors with a memorable and educational experience.

Uncover the ticket prices for the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham Alabama

When planning your visit to the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham, Alabama, it is essential to be aware of the ticket prices. The cost of admission varies depending on various factors such as age, residency, and time of visit. For adults (age 18 and above), the regular entrance fee is $15, while seniors (age 65 and above) can enjoy a discounted rate of $12. Children aged 3-12 are charged $10, and infants (under 3) enter free of charge. It is important to note that these prices are for non-residents. Alabama residents receive a reduced fee for admission, with rates of $10 for adults, $8 for seniors, and $7 for children aged 3-12. It is highly recommended to check the official website or contact the Botanical Gardens directly for the most up-to-date pricing information.

Apart from regular admission, the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham also offer various membership options that provide unlimited access and additional benefits. Individual annual memberships are available for $50, while family memberships can be purchased for $75, allowing two adults and their children under 18 to enjoy unlimited visits. The Gardens also offer special rates for students, military personnel, and educators. Additionally, group rates are available for 10 or more individuals, making it an excellent option for schools, organizations, or larger groups planning a visit. By considering these different ticket options and pricing, visitors can plan and budget accordingly, ensuring a delightful and affordable experience at the Botanical Gardens in Birmingham, Alabama.
